About Us

Welcome to Go Dive Mirissa

Welcome to Go Dive Mirissa, your gateway to unforgettable ocean adventures in Mirissa, Sri Lanka! We specialize in Scuba Diving, Turtle Snorkeling, Whale & Dolphin Watching Tours, and Boat Tours, offering you the chance to explore the breathtaking marine life of the Indian Ocean.

Join us and discover the magic beneath the waves—swim with turtles, encounter majestic whales, and dive into the beauty of Mirissa’s underwater world. Your next adventure starts here!
